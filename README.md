## ⚛️ What is TrezoaChain?

> **The quantum threat is not theoretical — it's an engineering timeline.**
> Every blockchain using ECDSA today (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana) will be breakable by quantum computers.
> TrezoaChain is the first Layer-1 built **quantum-resistant from day one**.

TrezoaChain implements **NIST-approved CRYSTALS-Kyber and CRYSTALS-Dilithium** directly at the protocol level — not as a plugin, not as a layer on top — as the cryptographic foundation of the entire chain.

## 🔬 Why Post-Quantum Cryptography?

**"Harvest Now, Decrypt Later"** — adversaries are collecting encrypted blockchain transactions today, planning to decrypt them when quantum computers are powerful enough.

NIST finalized post-quantum standards in 2024:

| Standard | Algorithm | Use in TrezoaChain |
|:---|:---|:---|
| FIPS 203 | CRYSTALS-Kyber | Key Encapsulation (KEM) |
| FIPS 204 | CRYSTALS-Dilithium | Digital Signatures |

Every other major L1 is vulnerable. TrezoaChain is not.
